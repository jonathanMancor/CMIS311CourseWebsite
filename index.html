Course Overview

This course provides an overview of essential skills for customer support and technical service professionals, emphasizing the importance of excellent communication, technical expertise, and effective stress management. It covers key areas such as achieving high customer satisfaction, mastering telephone and technical writing skills, and effectively handling challenging customer interactions.

Additionally, the course addresses the need for incident management and problem-solving techniques to streamline support processes and prevent recurring issues. Business related skills, including project management and understanding business metrics, is also important for advancing in technical roles. Teamwork within service desk settings is explored, emphasizing the value of collaboration and mutual support among team members.

Overall, the course prepares support professionals to excel in dynamic service environments, manage work-related stress, and develop career-enhancing skills.
